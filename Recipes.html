Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine flour, salt, and yeast mixture. Knead for 5 minutes.
  4. Roll out dough to 1/4 inch thickness.
  5. Spread pizza sauce, mozzarella cheese, and parmesan cheese on the dough.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until crust is golden brown.

Chicken Roll Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 8 slices cheddar cheese
  • 8 large flour tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Pound the chicken breasts between two sheets of plastic wrap until 1/4 inch thick. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika.
  2. Dredge the chicken in flour, dip in beaten eggs, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  3. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Fry the breaded chicken for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
  4. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each tortilla. Top with a breaded chicken breast.
  5. Roll up the tortilla tightly around the chicken and cheese. Secure with toothpicks.
  6. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to eat.

Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ghee or vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and cook until translucent.
  2. Add garlic, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, cardamom, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add rice and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover. Simmer for 15-20 minutes or until rice is cooked.
  5. Fluff with a fork and garnish with cilantro.

Bhindi Masala Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb okra, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and cook until translucent.
  2. Add garlic, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, cardamom, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add okra and cook until tender.
  4. Add tomato puree, lemon juice, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Simmer for 5-7 minutes or until the sauce thickens.
  6. Garnish with cilantro.

Fish Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fish fillets, cut into chunks
  • 2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tamarind balls, soaked in 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tsp coconut oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 10 curry leaves

Instructions:

  1. Grind the mustard seeds, fenugreek, garlic, ginger, peppercorns, chili powder, turmeric, coriander, and salt into a paste using 1/2 cup water.
  2. Add the fish, ground paste, tamarind water, and 1 cup water to a pot. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a separate pan, heat coconut oil. Fry the onion until golden brown. Add curry leaves.
  4. Pour the onion-curry leaf mixture into the fish curry. Simmer for 5 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
